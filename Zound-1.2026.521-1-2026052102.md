### Dictation & Recording

- Fixed a startup issue where dictation or recording could stop working after a previously selected microphone was unplugged or unavailable.
- Improved selected microphone recording so quiet starts and short pauses no longer make Zound think the microphone is unusable.
- Made hold-to-talk dictation finish more reliably, including keeping the last words when the network or transcription service is slow to respond.

### Dictionary & Transcription Accuracy

- Added a dedicated terms list in Dictionary settings for names, product names, acronyms, and phrases you want Zound to recognize.
- You can paste multiple terms at once, and Zound will turn them into editable tags automatically.
- Long background notes and important terms are now managed separately, making it easier to tune transcription without rewriting your whole dictionary.

### Stability & Polish

- Improved recovery when a dictation session is interrupted, so Zound can stop cleanly and be ready for the next dictation.
- Localized the new dictionary controls in Simplified Chinese.
